Mount Carmel Hospital

  • Location: Grove City, Ohio, United States
  • Budget: $250 million
  • Contractor: AECOM
  • Subcontractor: Kuwait Construction
  • Land acquisition: Mount Carmel Hospital is located on a 40-acre site that was acquired from the Catholic Church.

All of the 210 private patient rooms in the 504,241-square-foot, seven-story hospital will be inside. It will have a lot of different services, like surgery, interventional cardiology, an intensive care unit (ICU), a lab, a pharmacy, obstetrics, a neonatal intensive care unit (NICU), short stays or observation, and imaging. A five-story, 125,498-square-foot medical office building will connect to the hospital and have services for general care, maternal and fetal medicine, outpatient oncology, and women's health.

Construction

The construction of Mount Carmel Hospital marked a significant milestone in healthcare infrastructure, embodying a commitment to providing state-of-the-art medical facilities. The project commenced with meticulous planning and architectural considerations aimed at creating a cutting-edge healthcare institution. Construction crews worked diligently to bring the vision to life, erecting a modern and purposeful structure equipped with the latest advancements in medical technology.

The facility's design prioritized patient comfort and efficient workflow for medical professionals, incorporating spacious rooms, advanced diagnostic centers, and specialized treatment areas.

Technology Used

While the discussion thus far has centered on the selection of the land for Mount Carmel Hospital based on strategic geographic considerations, it is essential to delve into the technological aspects associated with this decision-making process. The utilization of technology played a crucial role in the assessment of various factors contributing to the selection of the hospital's location.

Geospatial technology and Geographic Information System (GIS) applications likely played a pivotal role in evaluating the accessibility and proximity of the chosen plot to essential amenities.
